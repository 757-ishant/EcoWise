About EcoWise

EcoWise leverages cutting-edge deep learning technology to classify waste materials accurately and efficiently. Our system is trained on over 15,000 images across 12 waste categories.

We aim to promote sustainable waste management practices by making recycling easier and more accessible through AI-powered image recognition.
